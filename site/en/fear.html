Market temperature (0 cold ~ 10 hot)

The headline "Total" reading averages smart-money positioning (all three institutional groups) with retail heat: when both lean long and active, the temperature runs hot; when both are short and quiet, it runs cold. You can also read each participant on its own dial — foreign, domestic or retail — and switch between gauge (latest close) and trend (last six months). It is a state reading of market sentiment and participation, not a trade signal.

Retail heat

The average of three 252-day percentile ranks — retail spot absorption, change in margin balance, and overall turnover — mapped to 0 (cold) to 10 (hot).

Smart-money positioning

The combined net position (spot + futures) of institutions ranked against its own 252-day history and mapped to 1 (most short) to 10 (most long). A structural-bias indicator, not a trade signal.
